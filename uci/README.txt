A selection of 8 high-dimensional datasets available at the UCI
(University of California, Irvine)
[Machine Learning Repository](http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/).

Note that their original purpose is for testing classification,
not clustering algorithms.


Gagolewski M., A framework for benchmarking clustering algorithms,
SoftwareX 20:101270, 2022. DOI: 10.1016/j.softx.2022.101270.
URL: <https://clustering-benchmarks.gagolewski.com/>.
